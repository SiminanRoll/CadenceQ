A simple rhythm your team will actually use

The point is not to add more admin work. The point is to make prospecting feel lighter, faster, and more repeatable while still giving managers the visibility they need.

1

Work the queue

Reps open CadenceQ and immediately see the highest-priority actions for the day, including untouched accounts, follow-ups, and due tasks.

2

Complete the touch

Each activity maps to the current stage in the cadence so the next move is obvious. No guessing. No reinventing the process.

3

Advance or hand off

Once the account is engaged and ready, schedule the next milestone, mark the outcome, and move it out of active follow-up the clean way.
